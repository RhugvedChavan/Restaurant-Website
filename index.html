Our story

A kitchen built around one fire, not a menu of them.

Ember & Oak opened in 2019 with a single rule: if it can't be cooked over the hearth, it doesn't go on the plate. That constraint shaped everything — a menu that changes with what the fire and the season allow, a dining room angled so every table can see the flame, and a kitchen that closes when the wood runs low, not when the clock says so.

We work directly with a small circle of growers and fishers across Maharashtra. What arrives in the morning decides what you eat at night.
